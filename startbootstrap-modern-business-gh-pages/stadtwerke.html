Challenges for Stadtwerke

Heterogeneous Systems

Stadtwerke have to manage various energy sources and taking the maximum out of every component of the network is becoming harder as the system include heterogeneous renewable resources.

Unpredictable Demand Peak

For Stadtwerke a proper planning is crucial to deal with unexpected demand peaks. Especially because of oscillations in the renewable energy supply.

High Operational Cost

Operational cost can easily bicome bigger than planning if the planning have not included the best cost-effective components in terms of operativity.
